# UPSC GS Research Notes

## What I collected

This folder does **not** include full downloaded text from copyrighted commercial books.
I used:

- Official UPSC previous-paper pages
- Official NCERT textbook portal for free textbooks
- Public government and institutional sources for current-affairs themes
- A conservative list of commonly recommended standard GS books gathered from public UPSC-prep references

## Important limitation

The request to "download all 50 years UPSC General Studies Questions" cannot be completed in a clean, fully official way from the public UPSC site alone, because the public UPSC archive does not expose a simple 50-year Civil Services GS dump in one place. I therefore saved the **official source pages** you can use for authenticated paper access and built the quiz from the last-decade UPSC pattern plus standard GS sources.

## Last 10 years current-affairs topic map

This is a **topic map**, not a full raw current-affairs dump. It is designed to reflect the kind of themes UPSC has repeatedly touched in the last decade.

### 2016

- GST constitutional and tax-reform transition
- Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code
- Paris Agreement follow-through and climate commitments

### 2017

- GST rollout and cooperative federalism
- BHIM-UPI / digital payments expansion
- Doklam and neighbourhood-security themes

### 2018

- MSP, agrarian distress, PM-AASHA debates
- Data protection debate after *Puttaswamy*
- Ayushman Bharat launch

### 2019

- Article 370 and Jammu & Kashmir reorganisation
- Citizenship debate and federal responses
- Jal Shakti, water stress, groundwater governance

### 2020

- Pandemic governance, health infrastructure, vaccine policy
- Atmanirbhar Bharat and supply-chain resilience
- Farm laws debate and agricultural marketing

### 2021

- COP26, net-zero discussions, climate finance
- National Monetisation Pipeline, infrastructure and logistics
- Digital economy regulation and platform governance

### 2022

- Russia-Ukraine war, food-energy-fertiliser security
- Semiconductors, supply-chain diversification
- Mission LiFE and green transitions

### 2023

- G20 India Presidency and New Delhi Leaders' Declaration
- Chandrayaan-3 and Aditya-L1
- Women's Reservation Act
- New criminal laws
- Digital Personal Data Protection Act
- Global Biofuel Alliance
- Green Hydrogen Mission

### 2024

- IndiaAI Mission
- Implementation debates around DPDP framework
- Public digital infrastructure and DPI export model
- Wetlands, forests, cheetah/tiger conservation, climate adaptation

### 2025

- Draft DPDP Rules and digital-governance implementation
- IndiaAI compute and indigenous AI ecosystem
- IMF growth outlook, trade uncertainty, macro resilience
- Environmental reporting, forest cover, carbon sink, clean-energy transitions

## How the quiz was designed

The quiz in `upsc_gs_quiz_50.md` is built to mirror recurring UPSC GS patterns from roughly the last 10 years:

- Statement-based elimination
- Static + current-affairs fusion
- Polity, economy, geography, environment, history, science-tech balance
- India-specific institutional and constitutional focus
- Conservation, mapping, governance and scheme-based questioning
